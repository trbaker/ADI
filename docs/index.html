ADI

ArcGIS Data Impact

Search for your ArcGIS Online Organization prefix (must be a U.S. K12 account). The prefix is the part of your URL in bold:
http://myschoolname.maps.arcgis.com



If your school is not in the system or you entered the prefix incorrectly, you'll receive a rather unfortunate 404 error. We're keeping things simple here. ADI attempts to document school-based ArcGIS Online sites with public "browsing enabled". Public maps and services are also discoverable through these pages.
